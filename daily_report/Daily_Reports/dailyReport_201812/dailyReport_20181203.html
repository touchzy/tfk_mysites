Milk eggs, fresh vegetables and fruits can help reduce uric acid. (4) Eat more alkaline food, alkaline urine, is conducive to the excretion of uric acid. "Four Less": (1) Drink less alcohol, especially beer should be less, even not to drink; (2) eat less food rich in purine, such as animal viscera (liver, brain, kidney, etc.), seafood; (3) eat less hot pot, do not drink thick broth;
