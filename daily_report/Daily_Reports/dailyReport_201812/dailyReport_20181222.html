The cause of osteoporosis is mainly caused by low bone mineral content, bone structure destruction, and decreased bone strength. Many people think that the way to prevent osteoporosis is to supplement calcium, which is actually wrong. Calcium supplementation does not mean prevention of osteoporosis. Calcium deficiency is just one of the causes of osteoporosis. Calcium eats a lot, not necessarily absorbed more, calcium absorption requires ample exercise and sunlight.
