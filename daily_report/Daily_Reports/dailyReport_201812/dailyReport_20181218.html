If the body lacks these nutrients, it will affect sugar, lipid metabolism and energy consumption. Serious cases may also lead to atherosclerosis. In addition, a long-term vegetarian diet will lead to a lack of protein, iron, zinc and other nutrients, resulting in nutritional imbalance. Faced with "three high" patients, doctors will recommend light diet, the correct diet standard is less oil, less salt, less sugar, which is not completely vegetarian can solve.
